# DeskHog is here

We're excited to announce the launch of **DeskHog**, our open-source developer toy that brings joy and creativity to your desk.

## What is DeskHog?

DeskHog is a collection of small, interactive devices that sit on your desk and provide delightful moments throughout your day. Each device has its own personality and purpose:

- **The Amber Device**: Features simple games and interactive elements
- **The Teal Device**: Displays friendly faces and expressions
- **The Clear Device**: Offers utility functions and notifications

## Why We Built It

As developers, we spend countless hours at our desks. We wanted to create something that would:
- Bring a smile to your face during long coding sessions
- Provide gentle reminders and notifications
- Serve as conversation starters with colleagues
- Demonstrate the joy of building physical things

## Open Source

DeskHog is completely open source, meaning you can:
- Build your own devices
- Modify the software
- Create new features
- Share your creations with the community

## Getting Started

To get started with DeskHog:

1. Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/your-org/deskhog)
2. Follow the build instructions
3. Customize your device
4. Share your modifications
